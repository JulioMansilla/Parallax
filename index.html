The land

In a world full of magic...

In a world full of magic, there was a nomadic castle. With steam-powered mechanical legs the abomination roamed the planes, searching to kill its hunger.

The inside

Welcome to our kitchen...

As the hungry victims walk in, driven by the inviting warmth of the kitchen's fireplace they meet Calsifire.

What an interesting flame, not only does he talk but he is also quite the host. Would you like some hot tea? Maybe a full English breakfast? Oh, poor thing, when was the last time you ate? When was the last time I ate...

Death!

As the question leaves his fiery mouth

His voice and appearance change, no longer do you see a wonderful little flame. Calsifire bulks up, no longer hiding his intentions. What follows is just as horrible as you are imagining. The flames engulf everything in his path and there is only the smell of burnt flesh and fat. The flames eventually subside, and Calsifire talks to the astonished Victim. From this day on, my curse will follow you and your bacon will ALWAYS BURN.
